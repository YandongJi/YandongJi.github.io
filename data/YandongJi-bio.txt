Yandong Ji is a visiting researcher at MIT, where he works on
legged robots and reinforcement learning. He received an MEng in Mechnical Engineering
from the University of California, Berkeley in 2022 advised by
Koushil Sreenath. Before that he received a BEng in Intelligent Science and Technology from
Nankai University in 2021 working with Juanjuan Zhang and Xian Guo.
